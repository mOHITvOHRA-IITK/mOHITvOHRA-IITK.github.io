About Me

I completed my PhD from IIT Kanpur on "Smart Manipulation Strategies for Autonomous Robotic Systems". My doctoral thesis is available here.

Currently, I am an Assistant Principal Researcher at Dubai Electricity and Water Authority (DEWA) – R&D, where I work on the design and deployment of field-ready autonomous robotic systems for real-world infrastructure applications.

Research Areas

  • GNSS-deprived Localization and SLAM
  • Autonomous Inspection Systems
  • Sensor–Robot Integration
  • Point Cloud Processing and 6D Pose Estimation
  • GNSS / RTK-based Localization
