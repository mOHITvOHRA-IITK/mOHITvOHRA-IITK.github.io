Mohit Vohra

Robotics researcher specializing in autonomous systems, machine vision, and GNSS/RTK-based localization.

About Me

Mohit Vohra completed his PhD from IIT Kanpur on "Smart Manipulation Strategies for Autonomous Robotic Systems". You can access the thesis here. Currently, he is an Assistant Principal Researcher at DEWA R&D (Robotics), focusing on multi-sensor perception, GNSS/RTK integration, autonomous inspection, and field-deployable robotic systems.

Current Role & Responsibilities

  • Leading development of multi-sensor perception systems for autonomous robots
  • Integrating GNSS/RTK and sensor data for precise localization
  • Designing and validating lab prototypes and field-deployable robotic systems
  • Collaborating with cross-functional teams on innovative robotic applications

Research Interests

  • Machine Vision
  • GNSS-deprived Localization (SLAM)
  • Autonomous Inspection
  • Sensor-Robot Integration
  • Point Cloud Processing
  • GNSS/RTK

Hobbies & Interests

I enjoy exploring new robotics technologies, reading research papers, coding, and experimenting with autonomous systems. Outside work, I enjoy photography, trekking, and traveling to learn about new cultures.
