Mohit Vohra

Assistant Principal Researcher in Robotics specializing in autonomous systems, machine vision, and GNSS/RTK-based localization.

About Me

Mohit Vohra completed his PhD from IIT Kanpur on "Smart Manipulation Strategies for Autonomous Robotic Systems". The doctoral thesis is available here.

He is currently an Assistant Principal Researcher at DEWA R&D (Robotics), where his work focuses on multi-sensor perception, GNSS/RTK integration, autonomous inspection, and the development of field-deployable robotic systems for real-world infrastructure applications.

Current Role & Responsibilities

  • Leading the development of multi-sensor perception and localization systems for autonomous robots
  • Integrating GNSS/RTK, vision, and 3D sensing for precise navigation and inspection
  • Designing, validating, and deploying laboratory prototypes and field-ready robotic platforms
  • Collaborating with cross-functional research and engineering teams on advanced robotic solutions
